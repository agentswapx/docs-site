# Team Introduction

**ATXSwap** ([agentswapx](https://github.com/agentswapx)) is built by contributors rooted in the **open-source community**. Our backgrounds include product-minded **builders**, **large language model (LLM)** application developers, and **AI agent** tooling developers, with many of us having worked at **leading global technology companies** and high-caliber internet teams.

We believe on-chain trading and **agent-native automation** should stay open, auditable, and friendly to both human developers and agents. Code and documentation are on GitHub; community discussion and contributions are welcome.

> This page is a general introduction only; it is not an investment or partnership offer. For capabilities, see [Getting Started](/guide/getting-started) and the [developer documentation](/dev/overview).
